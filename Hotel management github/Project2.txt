This Project is developed using Tkinter(python) for GUI, MySQL for Database connectivity.

Options :

Check-in- It takes the entry of all the required customer details such as customer name, the phone number of  customer
mode of payment, type of the bedroom, the check-in date and the check-out date and
finally the room number alloted to the customer.
This information is then stored in MySQL database

Customer details- It is used to display the details of the customers that have checked-in in the hotel.
The table present in MySQL database is printed in this window.

Check out- The details of Customer who is checking out is asked.
The information stored in the table also gets deleted after check-out is completed.
